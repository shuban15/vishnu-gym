Workout Splits

Push Day

Focus on chest, shoulders, and triceps. Great for building upper body strength.

Pull Day

Targets back and biceps. Helps improve posture and pulling power.

Leg Day

Build strength and endurance in your lower body with squats and lunges.

Beginner's Tips

  • Start light and focus on proper form before adding weight.
  • Stay consistent — small progress every week matters.
  • Drink enough water and rest between workout sessions.
  • Include warm-ups and stretching to prevent injuries.
  • Track your workouts to stay motivated and see growth.
